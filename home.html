Indian Railways Ticket Booking (for Ordinary and Disabled Persons)             


Purpose of Our Website

This website aims to provide an easy and efficient ticket booking system for passengers, suffering from Cancer and from Disabilities (like Unstable mental state, etc.) traveling on Indian Railways. We focus on delivering a user-friendly interface for both ordinary and disabled persons to access railway tickets with ease.

The disabled person should have Unique disability card so that we can be verify that using registered phone number, only after that the person get discount to Railway tickets. A chart is also available at the top-right corner to check discount criteria.
